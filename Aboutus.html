OUR STORY

About Us

GrowMore Freight LLC is a leading logistics brokerage company dedicated to connecting shippers with reliable carriers to streamline freight transportation nationwide.

WHO WE ARE

Bridging the Gap in Logistics

Founded in 2013, GrowMore Freight has established itself as a trusted partner in the logistics industry, providing innovative freight brokerage solutions that connect shippers with reliable carriers across the nation.

Our mission is to streamline the logistics process, making it more efficient, transparent, and cost-effective for businesses of all sizes. By leveraging cutting-edge technology and industry expertise, we've built a robust platform that simplifies freight transportation.

OUR JOURNEY

Company Milestones

From our humble beginnings to our position as an industry leader, explore the key moments in our company's history.

2013

Company Founded

GrowMore Freight was established with a small team of logistics professionals dedicated to improving the freight brokerage experience.

2015

Technology Platform Launch

We introduced our proprietary logistics platform, connecting shippers and carriers through an intuitive digital interface.

2017

National Expansion

GrowMore expanded operations to serve all 48 contiguous states, establishing regional offices to better support our growing carrier network.

2019

Service Diversification

We broadened our service offerings to include specialized freight, temperature-controlled shipping, and international logistics solutions.
