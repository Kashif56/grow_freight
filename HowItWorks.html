Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our logistics process and services.

For standard shipments, we typically require 24-48 hours' notice to optimize routing and resource allocation. However, we understand that urgent situations arise, and our expedited service can accommodate same-day pickups in many locations. Contact our logistics team immediately for urgent shipment needs, and we'll work to find the fastest solution.

Our comprehensive tracking system provides real-time location updates, estimated arrival times, and progress notifications. You'll receive automated alerts at key milestones: pickup confirmation, departure, checkpoint crossings, and approaching delivery. Our customer portal provides 24/7 access to current status, route visualization, and delivery documentation.

We specialize in accommodating diverse freight needs, including temperature-controlled transport, hazardous materials, oversized loads, and fragile items. During the initial consultation, please inform us of any special requirements so we can assign appropriate equipment and personnel. Our team is certified in various specialized handling procedures to ensure your cargo receives the exact care it needs.

We proactively monitor potential disruptions like weather events or traffic issues and immediately develop alternative routing when needed. If a delay occurs, your dedicated coordinator will contact you promptly with the cause, revised delivery estimate, and mitigation plan. Our contingency protocols include backup vehicles, alternative routes, and priority rescheduling to minimize impact on your operations.

All shipments include basic cargo liability coverage, but we recommend our comprehensive insurance options for full protection. Standard coverage includes protection against carrier negligence, while our premium coverage extends to all risk factors including natural disasters and theft. We can tailor insurance levels based on cargo value and risk profile, and all claims are processed through our streamlined system with typical resolution within 10-15 business days.
