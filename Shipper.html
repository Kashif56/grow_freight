COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our shipping services and processes.

You can request a quote through our online form, by calling our customer service team, or by emailing us with your shipment details. We'll need information such as origin and destination, freight dimensions, weight, commodity type, and any special requirements. Our team will provide a competitive quote promptly.

We handle a wide range of freight types including general freight, palletized goods, machinery, construction materials, retail goods, food products (non-perishable and temperature-controlled), hazardous materials (with proper documentation), and oversized loads. Our network includes carriers with various equipment types to accommodate diverse shipping needs.

Once your shipment is booked and assigned to a carrier, you'll receive tracking information via email. You can use our online tracking portal to monitor your shipment in real-time. Additionally, our logistics team provides regular updates on the status of your freight and can be contacted for specific information at any time during transit.

For standard shipments, you'll need to provide a bill of lading with accurate freight description, packaging details, and shipping/receiving contact information. For hazardous materials, additional documentation including proper classification and safety data sheets is required. For international shipments, customs documentation and commercial invoices are necessary. Our team will guide you through the specific requirements for your shipment.

All carriers in our network are required to maintain appropriate cargo insurance. Standard carrier liability typically covers $100,000 for full truckload and varies for LTL shipments. We recommend shippers verify if this coverage is sufficient for their goods. For high-value shipments, we can arrange additional insurance coverage at competitive rates to ensure your freight is fully protected.
